# Dashboard User Guide

## Overview

The Waruiru Farm Dashboard provides a centralized view of your farm's key metrics and quick access to essential features. It's designed to give you an at-a-glance understanding of your financial performance, inventory status, and other critical farm operations.

## Key Features

*   **Financial Overview:** See your total revenue and daily revenue trends.
*   **Order Tracking:** Monitor new orders and their status.
*   **Inventory Insights:** Get an overview of your inventory value and low-stock alerts.
*   **Quick Actions:** Access frequently used features directly from the dashboard.

## Navigating the Dashboard

Upon logging in, you will be directed to the dashboard. The main sections include:

### 1. Statistics Cards

At the top of the dashboard, you'll find a series of cards displaying aggregated statistics:
*   **Total Revenue:** The total income generated from sales.
*   **New Orders:** The number of new orders placed within a recent period (e.g., last 30 days).
*   **Inventory Value:** The total monetary value of your current stock.
*   **New Customers:** The number of new customers acquired within a recent period.

You can customize the visibility of these cards through your user preferences.

### 2. Recent Activity/Alerts

This section will display recent activities or important alerts, such as:
*   Low stock notifications.
*   New problem reports (if integrated).
*   Recent financial transactions.

### 3. Quick Access / Shortcuts

Dedicated links or buttons for frequently used features:
*   AI Crop Analysis (Farm Doctor Bot)
*   Inventory Management
*   Financial Reporting

## Customizing Your Dashboard (Preferences)

You can personalize your dashboard view by adjusting user preferences. This typically includes:
*   Toggling the visibility of different statistics cards.
*   Setting thresholds for alerts.

## Troubleshooting

*   **Data not updating:** Ensure your internet connection is stable. If the issue persists, contact support.
*   **Missing sections:** Check your user preferences to ensure all desired sections are enabled.
