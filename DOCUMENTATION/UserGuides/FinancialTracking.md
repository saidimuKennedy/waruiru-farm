# Financial Tracking User Guide

## Overview

The Financial Tracking section of Waruiru Farm provides comprehensive tools to monitor and analyze your farm's financial performance. You can view all transactions, track total revenue, and visualize daily revenue trends.

## Key Features

*   **Transaction List:** View a detailed list of all financial transactions.
*   **Total Revenue:** See your cumulative income.
*   **Daily Revenue Chart:** Visualize your revenue over time to identify trends.
*   **Financial Reports:** Generate and download reports for specific periods.

## Accessing Financial Tracking

To access Financial Tracking, navigate to the Dashboard and click on the "Financials" link or a similar shortcut. Alternatively, you can find it in the main navigation menu under "Financials" or "Reports".

## Using the Financial Tracking Tools

### 1. Transactions List

The transactions list displays every recorded financial transaction. Each entry typically includes:
*   **Date:** When the transaction occurred.
*   **M-Pesa Receipt:** The unique M-Pesa transaction ID (for mobile payments).
*   **Order ID:** The associated order.
*   **Amount:** The monetary value of the transaction.

You may be able to filter or sort this list based on various criteria.

### 2. Total Revenue

A prominent display will show your farm's total accumulated revenue. This figure is updated with every new transaction.

### 3. Daily Revenue Chart

A graphical representation of your daily revenue helps you understand performance trends. You can often adjust the time frame of the chart to view data over different periods (e.g., last 7 days, last 30 days, custom range).

### 4. Generating Financial Reports

To generate a financial report:
1.  Navigate to the Financial Tracking section.
2.  Look for a "Generate Report" or "Download Report" option.
3.  Specify a **Start Date** and **End Date** for the period you wish to report on.
4.  Choose the **Format** for your report (e.g., CSV, PDF).
5.  Click "Generate" or "Download" to save the report to your device.

The report will typically include:
*   Summary of the reporting period and total revenue.
*   A detailed list of all transactions within the specified period.

## Troubleshooting

*   **Missing transactions:** Ensure all payments are successfully processed. Check M-Pesa callbacks if applicable.
*   **Incorrect revenue figures:** Verify individual transaction amounts and ensure all relevant sales are being recorded.
*   **Report generation issues:** Check your date ranges and ensure the selected format is supported.
